- **Default language:** English (United States)

## Store listing copy

- **Short description** (<= 80 chars)
  Retreat convoy map, group chat, and live coordination in one place.

- **Full description**
  Calvary Caravan helps retreat groups travel together with clearer coordination and safer convoy visibility.

  What you can do:
  - Join a retreat with a simple invite code
  - Use phone-number identity continuity to reconnect your retreat profile
  - Share live location while using the app for convoy map visibility
  - Pause location sharing at any time in Profile
  - Send group chat updates and leader alerts
  - Add optional profile photo and vehicle details for quick recognition

  Privacy controls:
  - Location sharing can be turned off without leaving the retreat
  - In-app account deletion removes your retreat participant data
  - Support and policy pages are publicly available

  Calvary Caravan is focused on retreat travel coordination only. No advertising profile is built from your data.
