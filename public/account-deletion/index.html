Calvary Caravan Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from inside the app or by contacting support. Deletion removes your account data for Calvary Caravan and detaches your retreat profile.

In-app deletion

  1. Open Calvary Caravan.
  2. Go to your account/settings area.
  3. Choose Delete account and confirm.

API endpoint (for support-assisted deletion)

POST /api/v1/account/delete

If you need help, contact support at codehogg@gmail.com.

This page exists to satisfy app-store account-deletion policy disclosure requirements.
